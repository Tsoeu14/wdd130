South Africa

South Africa is a democratic country located at the tip of the southern hemisphere of the African continent. Its a blend of diverse cultural ideology and modern influence and is mostly referred to as the Rainbow Nation. It is home to many diverse and beautiful wildlife and amazing landscape, making it a great travel destination.
