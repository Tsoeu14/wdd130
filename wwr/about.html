Come along with us to do some team-building, exercise and sporty competition.

History

It all started with a family of competitors. They played every game and sport possible. And after realizing that there was a river near their property, the decided to go and try something new there. After creating make-shift boats they then saw how nice it is to flow with the river. This sparked the idea of starting a pro rafting business now called Fun and Exciting Water Adventures.
