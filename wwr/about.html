History

Matthew van Merwe is the founder of Tumbling waters and he found this land in 1988. It was originally a family vacation home and after a few years of them not knowing what to do with this massive property, Mat saw an opportunity to invite more people and fill the place.

He wanted people to gather together and have the best time. He then invested in building the company and it covers the river and the woods. The site now has campsites, hiking and quard-biking trails. There are so many recreational activities for people to do when the come to Tumbling Waters.
