THE TURNING TIDES!

At The Turing Tides we believe that adventure begins where the river bends. With expert guides,top tier safety standards and breathtaking routes through SSouth Africa's wildest waters we offer unforgettable rafting experiences for explorers,families and nature lovers. Whether your chasing the adrenaline or soaking in the scenery we are here to make your experience extraordinary.

History

South Africa has one of the world's best water rafting sites. They were first discovered by rich/upper class families who used to go to the rivers to relax. A man called Shier saw this an an opportunity to get competitive. He had to show them he's the best.

He challenged his neighbors to see who could survive the rapids and sail through them to the bottom of the river. This then led to other residents wanting to join in this fun. He then saw a business opportunity and that is how The Turning Tides was founded.
